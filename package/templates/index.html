How it works
1 SUBMIT TRIP DETAILS

Submit your tour ideas and thoughts. Our trip questionnaire will ask some key questions that you need to consider when we help plan the perfect tour package for you.

2 CONNECT WITH A TRAVEL EXPERT

Based on your trip input, we will connect you with an expert trip designer and architect who has first-hand, local knowledge of your trip destination(s). Together, you will build the perfect trip, suiting your trip ideas and requirements.

3 SECURELY BOOK YOUR TRIP

Once the overall tour, including accommodations, level of activity, daily itinerary has been customized to your liking, you can securely book your trip.

Advantages of customized tours
LOCAL INSIGHT

The expert trip designer will have first-hand, local insight and knowledge about your trip destinations, allowing them to give you a memorable, meaningful trip experience.

PERSONALIZATION

The trip duration, itinerary, type of accommodation, level of activity, budget etc. will be all be personalized per your requirements, ensuring that we are building a trip that 100% matches your needs.

TRAVEL LIKE A PRO

Think of our trip designers as your ticket backstage. Special access, favourable prices and local insight. Their role is to make sure that your entire trip is perfectly planned and run in a professional, secure and stress-free manner.
