    FAQ

    Is my payment secure?

    Yes, your payment is secure. We apply the latest payment security standards including encryption of your data. We also apply a security standard called SSL and only work with the acknowledged payment provider Stripe.

    Can I make changes after I book?

    Yes, and we would like to help you. If you wish to adjust the number of people, change your contact details, or change start time or start date, please contact us at: customerservice@bookmundi.com

    For booking cancellations, please check the relevant cancellation term before booking. They will be shown clearly on all our tours.

    Are there any hidden charges?

    There are no hidden charges, and no fees added. The price you see is the price pay, and nothing more. Bookmundi also covers the credit card processing fees.

    What can I expect after booking?

    You can expect a hassle-free tour. After booking, we will send you your booking confirmation and voucher via email. With this in your hand you can embark on the tour booked at the stipulated start date.
